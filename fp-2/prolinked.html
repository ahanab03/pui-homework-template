Problem Space

Immigrants face tough job market hurdles. Stats show they're 2.6 times more likely to be unemployed than US-born individuals. Immigrant income as of 2023 is 20-25% lower than their US-born counterparts, underscoring a significant wage gap.

In response, I developed ProLinked, an app connecting immigrants with mentors for tailored job market guidance.

Interviews

Gain a better understanding on why some immigrants believe the job searching process to be unjust.

  • - 8 interviews with immigrants currently working in the US
  • - 2 interviews with immigrants searching for jobs in the US

“I do not know English very well and interviewing in another language is hard”

“I often get told that I don't have the right experience. I don't know what that means”

“I went to a good school in India, but people don't trust my degree”

“Back in my hometown, there was no need for a Cover Letter or References”

Homepage Mockup

Color: Blue

- Represents calmness and stability

- Want to be reassuring towards new users

- Be a consistent and reliable app for users who hope to develop their skills


Bottom Tab Bar

- 49% of the time customers will use right thumb to navigate mobile apps, making a bottom nav bar more reachable

- Encourages scrolling

- Keeps the customer curious to explore all features as opposed to sandwich menu


Icons

- 47% of US immigrants are not proficient in English

- Making use of icons to provide information in a universal manner

- Easily understood by all ages


Horizontal Scrolling

- Gives most amount of information

- Provides user with a choice of what to explore

- Changes to vertical scrolling based on which category is explored
